Movie Name: Patna Se Pakistan

About:

The movie is a Bhojpuri action-drama based on terrorism and revenge. Kabir's family is killed in a terrorist attack in Patna. After not getting help from politicians and the system, he decides to take revenge himself. He travels to Pakistan to fight the terrorists responsible for his family's death.
